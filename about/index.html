About the Artist

Who is Shivam Madaan?

Shivam Madaan is a 24-year-old guitarist, music producer, composer, songwriter, singer, and musical artist from Delhi, India.

Known for his fingerstyle guitar playing and indie-pop releases, Shivam blends acoustic emotion with modern musical textures to create songs that feel personal, melodic, and honest.

The Story

A musical journey built around emotion, storytelling, and evolving sound.

Shivam started shaping his sound through guitar-led arrangements, expressive melodies, and carefully produced indie-pop ideas. His music carries a personal tone, often combining acoustic guitar or piano with subtle electronic textures.

He made his professional debut in 2022 and has since released multiple tracks while collaborating with other artists and steadily building a presence across streaming and social platforms.

His work reflects a balance between raw musicality and modern production, keeping the voice, melody, and emotion at the center of each release.
