Who is Shivam Madaan?

Shivam Madaan is a 24-year-old musical artist from Delhi, India, known for his fingerstyle playing and indie-pop releases.

He made his professional debut in 2022 and has since released multiple tracks, collaborating with other artists while steadily building a loyal audience across streaming and social platforms.
